Visit Safi!

Safi is a coastal city in Morocco, famous for its pottery, fishing port, and Atlantic beaches.

Things to do in Safi

Tranquil Beach

Experience the serene beauty of Safi's beaches under the stars.

Historic Cityscape

Explore the ancient castle of Safi and learn about its rich history.

Fishing Port

Discover the bustling fishing port of Safi, where you can watch local fishermen at work and enjoy fresh seafood.
